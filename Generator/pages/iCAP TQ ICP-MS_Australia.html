Introducing the iCAP TQ ICP-MS
     

The first, future-proof triple quadrupole ICP-MS solution to deliver enhanced performance and ease of use for both demanding routine analysis and challenging research applications.

The Thermo Scientific™ iCAP™ TQ ICP-MS combines triple quadrupole accuracy with single quadrupole ease of use, removing interferences for better quality data in challenging samples. Easily switch between single and triple quadrupole modes in one method depending on your analytes. Intuitive software makes it very easy to use, just choose your elements, and let the software do the rest.

  • Ease of Use - Harness the power of triple quadrupole technology with surprising ease of use
  • Reaction Finder - Simplified method development for the most challenging analytical applications
  • Right First Time - Accurate analysis and powerful interference removal - even in the toughest matrices
  • Boundless Capabilities - Push the boundaries of your research with ultra-low detection limits
